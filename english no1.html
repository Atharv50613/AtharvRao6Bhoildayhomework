Introduction

Ila Sachani is an inspiring individual whose life story is one of determination, resilience, and triumph over adversity. Born in a small town, Ila's journey is an example of how passion and hard work can lead to remarkable success. story highlights her early life, the challenges she faced, and the legacy she continues to build.

Early Life

Ila Sachani was born in the tranquil village of Rajkot, Amreli,Gujarat she was born in farmers famliy. Growing up in a modest household, she could not use her hands as they were small. She did not have devopled limbs. At small age she learnt to write using her legs, however she was slow at writing hence faced challenges in clearing her 10th standard board exam. She learnt to eat using her legs, do house hold chores like washing clothes, cleaning stuffs using her legs. Ila's family were skilled at Kathiawar embroidery and they encouraged the little girl to focus on art.
